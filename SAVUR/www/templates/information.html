Counseling Center
For confidential emotional support, schedule an appointment during normal business hours (Monday-Friday, 9:00AM-5:00PM)
SARC
To connect with someone 24/7 who can review all options available to a survivor of sexual assault (medical care, reporting, counseling) and who can accompany a student to campus and/or community services to ensure appropriate medical care, emotional support, and follow-up information in the wake of a sexual assault. If someone isn't sure whom to talk to, calling a SARC is a good way to explore options.
Student Health Center
To receive 24/7, confidential medical care (including pregnancy, predatory drug and sexually transmitted infection testing)
Public Safety
To report any act of sexual violence
Dean of Students
To pursue disciplinary action under the Code of Student Conduct
